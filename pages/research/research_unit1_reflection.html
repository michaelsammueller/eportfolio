Unit 1: Reflection

I have no experience with project management, so I was excited to finally get started and start learning about it. This module is based on team work, which will be a challenge once again - mainly because of schedule and time differences. I guess this is one of the challenges that have to be overcome, but it does make it extra challenging to study while working and travelling.

I've set up a WhatsApp group for me and my team mates, but I'm still waiting for one of my team members to respond, so I can add her to the group. We have started working on the team contract, which is due by the end of this week. Let's see how this goes. I was also part of a team for the "Secure Software Design" module, and that was a very enjoyable experience, mainly because we worked well together as a team. Let's hope it is the same this time around.
