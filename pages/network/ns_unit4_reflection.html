Unit 4: Reflection

Wow. I received a distinction for my last assignment. That's great! Now I can focus on the next two assignments - The "Executive Summary" for my vulnerability audit, and the individual reflection we need to write. I am really looking forward to using all these tools to scan the website and see which vulnerabilites I can find. It has always been on my mind, and, like mentioned before, I have used tools and websites before to gain understanding about this topic. I hope that, this time, I will keep at it and potentially earn some cybersecurity certifications as well.
