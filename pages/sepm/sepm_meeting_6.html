Summary - COOCMO Method for Project Cost Estimation

Michael presented his methods for calculating the total cost of software, hardware, and staff for a project. He mentioned a specific method using the COCOMO algorithm to estimate effor and cost based on lines of code. Tarek asked for clarification on the difference between total cost and total estimated cost, with Michael stating that the methods calculate the actual cost, but to also fulfill the "estimation" requirements, COCOMO is used to estimate cost. The team also discussed a system that matches staff members to components, considering factors such as available staff, cost, and working days. The group agreed that using a set of keywords to assign capabilities to components and staff members simplifies the process and helps with optimization.
