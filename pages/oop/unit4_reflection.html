Unit 4: Reflection

Unit 4 was great. What I learned in Unit 3 has now been substantiated by a lot of examples for every type of diagram and model. It allowed me to gain a thorough understanding of the various UML diagram types and when to use them, as well as what information they best represent. As touched upon in the lectures and the reading material, this will be very important for when we tackle our assignments later in this module, so I have ensured to pay extra attention to the concepts introduced in this model. The continous use of UML has also demonstrated how easy it is to use this concept for system design. Implementing classes designed in UML programmatically is very easy.
