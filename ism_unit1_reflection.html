Unit 1: Reflection

Unit 1 served as a great introduction to Information Security Management. I have never been involved in risk management before, as my job focuses mainly on practical programming and maintenance work on air traffic control simulators. As part of my job, I perform gap analyses whenever we attempt to replicate live operational systems. Upgrading both hardware and software does carry risks, which will be identified and mitigated throughout the upgrade process. Other than that, this topic is completely new to me, so I am excited to see what I will be able to learn from the material of this module.

This is also the first time I am participating in a 10 credit module, instead of a 20 credit module, so it will be interesting to how a shorter module compares to a longer one, especially when it comes to the assignments.
