Launching into Computer Science

Image by storyset on Freepik

This module provides a high-level introduction/overview of the topics covered in greater detail in other modules in the Master's programme, as well as aspects you will encounter during your career in Computer Science.

The intention of this module to explore and investigate the fundamental theories and practices of computing. You will then gradually explore the trends and current legislative, regulatory and ethical developments in the field of computer science, which are used to minimise computer technology related threats and personal data breaches.(University of Essex Online)
